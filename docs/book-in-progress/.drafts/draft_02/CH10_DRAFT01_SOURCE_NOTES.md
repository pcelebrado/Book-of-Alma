## Gaps and Limitations

1. **Copper-specific data:** While gold analysis is well-sourced from Alma's posts, copper-specific commentary was less detailed in the corpus. Section 10.3 relies on general industrial commodity dynamics that Alma referenced but did not detail exhaustively.

2. **Kurtosis statistical specifics:** Alma mentions kurtosis conceptually but does not provide detailed statistical analysis of distribution changes. Section 10.5 focuses on positioning dynamics (vomma) rather than pure statistical kurtosis.

3. **Term premium quantification:** Alma references term premium stress but does not provide specific term premium level estimates. Section 10.1 focuses on the mechanics and implications rather than quantified levels.

## Voice and Style Notes

- Maintained Alma's emphasis on measurable conditions preceding tail events
- Connected specific technical indicators (swap spreads, vomma, ISM components) to tail risk buildup
- Avoided predicting specific tail events; focused on conditions that make tails more likely
- Trading implications emphasize preparation rather than prediction
- Strong emphasis on regime identification and appropriate hedging for different shock types
