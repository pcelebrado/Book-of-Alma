### 3. Added Section 8.5: Geopolitical-Volatility Regime Taxonomy
Three comprehensive regime types with structured format:

**Regime Type I: The Escalation Cascade**
- What it looks like: Compounding uncertainty through narrative momentum
- What works: Long vol with extended horizons, correlation-aware hedging
- What fails: Quick mean-reversion, static hedges, binary event positioning
- Risk: Regime persists longer than positioning patience

**Regime Type II: The Stagflationary Squeeze**
- What it looks like: Simultaneous growth slowdown and inflation persistence
- What works: Real rate volatility, commodity exposure, pricing power factors
- What fails: 60/40 construction, duration-heavy fixed income, standard recession plays
- Risk: Policy errors from impossible central bank trade-offs

**Regime Type III: The Liquidity Fracture**
- What it looks like: Sudden liquidity withdrawals in funding markets
- What works: Maximum liquidity preference, options-based hedges
- What fails: Leveraged carry trades, complex multi-leg strategies, risk parity
- Risk: Gap risk that cannot be hedged with standard instruments

**Added:** Regime Boundaries and Overlap subsection covering transition dynamics and position sizing implications
