- **Location**: OP3NF1XER/nate-alma/book-in-progress/chapters/CH04.md:13
- **Issue**: "When customers buy options, dealers become short gamma" is not conditionally phrased. Customer flow is heterogeneous; dealer netting, option type, and maturities matter.
- **Required Fix**: Replace with conditional language: "When net customer flow leaves dealers synthetically short convexity (net short gamma), hedging becomes trend-following..." and reference the tooling/anti-superstition warning in bible/corpus/substack/unknown-date-liquidity-structure-let-s-put-speed-profile-into-context.md.

### MEDIUM: Mathematical section is too hand-wavy for a chapter titled "Gamma"

- **Issue**: The text claims "key insight is not exact formula" but then relies on proportionality statements. For reader safety, it needs one crisp, correct formula or a properly bounded approximation plus a statement of assumptions.
