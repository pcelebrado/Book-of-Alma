## Findings

### HIGH: Incorrect expectancy claim
- **Location**: C:/P4NTH30N/OP3NF1XER/nate-alma/book-in-progress/chapters/CH14.md#L13
- **Issue**: A 60% win rate with 1:1 average win/loss has positive expectancy before costs; it is not "zero expectancy".
- **Evidence**: "A system with a 60% win rate and 1:1 risk-reward has zero expectancy after costs."
- **Required Fix**: Replace with correct math using the expectancy formula (include costs explicitly): `E = p*W - (1-p)*L - costs`. State the condition for breakeven costs.

### HIGH: Alma attribution without Bible sourcing
- **Location**: C:/P4NTH30N/OP3NF1XER/nate-alma/book-in-progress/chapters/CH14.md#L21
- **Issue**: Repeated "Alma" attributions read like direct doctrine but are not cited.
- **Evidence**: "Alma's if-then discipline..."; "Alma tracks multiple windows of risk simultaneously".
- **Required Fix**: Either add Bible citations where Alma teaches these practices, or reframe as the author's synthesis without naming Alma.

### MEDIUM: Position sizing formula is oversimplified (especially for options)
- **Location**: C:/P4NTH30N/OP3NF1XER/nate-alma/book-in-progress/chapters/CH14.md#L79
- **Issue**: The formula assumes linear PnL vs spot move and ignores options greeks/path dependence, gap risk, and liquidity.
- **Evidence**: "Position Size = Risk Unit / Distance to Invalidation."
- **Required Fix**: Add explicit assumptions (spot instrument, hard stop fill, no gaps) and an options caveat (use delta/VaR/defined-risk structures).

### MEDIUM: Probability assignments guidance is too loose
- **Location**: C:/P4NTH30N/OP3NF1XER/nate-alma/book-in-progress/chapters/CH14.md#L51
- **Issue**: "Ordinal is sufficient" is fine, but the chapter should warn against false precision and confirm-update discipline.
- **Required Fix**: Add a brief calibration step (what evidence updates probabilities; what invalidates a scenario).
